                                                    Success Factors

                                                    1. Start with requirements: Understanding business needs is critical
                                                    2. Design for scale: Build for growth from day one
                                                    3. Monitor everything: Comprehensive observability is essential
                                                    4. Iterate and optimize: Continuous improvement approach
                                                    5. Document thoroughly: Knowledge preservation and transfer

                                                    Common Challenges

                                                    • Data quality issues: Require robust validation frameworks
                                                    • Performance at scale: Need careful optimization strategies
                                                    • Cost management: Cloud costs can spiral without optimization
                                                    • Security compliance: Complex regulatory requirements
                                                    • Legacy integration: Technical debt from existing systems
